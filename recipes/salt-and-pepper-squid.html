Salt and Pepper Squid

Description

Prepared in a similar manner to Cantonese Salt & Pepper Pork Chops, the fried pieces of calamari are tossed in a mixture of crunchy stir-fried garlic, ginger, and hot green peppers.

Ingredients

  • Squid
  • Shaoxing wine
  • Sesame oil
  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Prepare squid
  2. Marinate squid
  3. Heat oil
  4. Make dry mixture
  5. Cook squid in oil
  6. Stir-fry squid with the peppers
