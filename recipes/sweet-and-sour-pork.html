Sweet and Sour Pork

Description

Sweet and sour pork sits among some of the most well-known Chinese dishes outside of China. It's widely believed to be a Cantonese dish, known colloquially as gú lóuh yuhk, which roughly translates to “ancient pork”.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Peppers
  • Pineapples
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Oil
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cut and marinate pork
  2. Cut vegetables
  3. Make sauce
  4. Coat pork in flour
  5. Fry pork
  6. Cook other ingredients, sauce and mix
