Tomato and Eggs

Description

This dish is a weeknight favorite! You won't believe how much flavor and texture you can create in 15 minutes. Honestly, maybe even 10 if you're super speedy. Bonus: it requires the absolute bare minimum of ingredients. We don't even use garlic, or soy sauce, or oyster sauce. You can probably find every single one of these ingredients in any typical grocery store or supermarket.

Ingredients

  • Ketchup
  • Cornstarch
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes
  2. Prepare eggs and beat
  3. Cook eggs
  4. Cook tomatoes
  5. Add eggs and ketchup
